About Me

I’m Eman Fatima, a dedicated student at NUST, Islamabad, pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Materials Engineering. My academic background gives me a unique appreciation for structure, texture, and the finer details of how things are made, which surely influences my creative perspective.

Beyond engineering, I am a creator at heart. I love to learn and explore, constantly seeking intersections between technology, science, and art. Whether I'm analyzing material properties in a lab or composing a shot through my camera lens, I am driven by curiosity and a desire to understand the world around me.

My hobbies are a reflection of this curiosity. I am an avid photographer, always looking to capture fleeting moments and intricate details. I also express my creativity through various arts and crafts, finding joy in the tactile process of making tangible, beautiful objects.

I spend a lot of my free time watching documentaries, feeding my mind with new perspectives on history, nature, and innovation. For me, every day is an opportunity to learn something new and create something meaningful.
